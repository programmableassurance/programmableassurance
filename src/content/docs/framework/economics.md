# Governance Economics

Governance decisions have economic consequences.

Programmable Assurance makes those consequences visible.

Examples include:

- regulatory exposure
- contractual liability
- operational cost
- incident recovery
- reputational impact

Governance Economics translates technical decisions into organizational impact, enabling leaders to understand not only what happened, but what it means.
